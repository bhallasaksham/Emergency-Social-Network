Emergency Social Network

 

Welcome to Emergency Social Network!

Emergency Assist welcomes you to your one stop solution for all emergencies.
Ask for help with the click of a button and chat with your friends and peers.
Share your current status with friends and peers.



Statuses allowed:

Emergency: I need help now. This is a life threatening emergency!

Help: I need help, but this is not a life threatening emergency.

OK: I am OK. I do not need help.

This app is only intended for help during emergencies. Please refrain from sharing emergency status unnecessarily.
